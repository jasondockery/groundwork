# Groundwork Twelve — learning log

This repo is your progress artifact for [Groundwork Twelve](https://jasondockery.github.io/groundwork/groundwork-twelve.html):
the daily log, gate-check reviews, and every stage build live here. By the
twelfth gate it is a portfolio of proof, not a certificate.

## Use

1. Declare your pace at the top of `log.md` — steady (~1h/day), committed
   (2–4h/day), immersed (8–12h/day), or your own number. Change it whenever
   life changes; the gates don't care about the calendar.
2. One line in `log.md` per day — what you did, how it felt, time spent.
3. Commit every day, even minimum days. The streak is `git log`.
4. At each gate check, add a short review: what got easier, what still feels
   slow, one thing to repeat.
5. Stage builds get committed here too (or linked, once they grow into their
   own repos).
6. Want the plan served daily, sized to your real hours? Start your agent here
   and use `prompts/scheduler.md`. From Stage 4 on, run `prompts/examiner.md`
   at every gate.

Push this repo to GitHub if public accountability helps you — many people
finish because someone might be watching.

## Day modes (any pace)

- **Full day:** your pace, as planned.
- **Minimum day:** 15 minutes — one drill, one log line, one commit. Real day.
- **Catch-up day:** yesterday's material plus today's. Never stack more than
  one missed day.
